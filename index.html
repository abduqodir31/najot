  • 1937 -Finishes university and takes a job in

  • 1938 -Marries wife of 69 years Marget

  • 1942 -Receives a Ph.D. in Geneticts and

  • 1944 -Reject a 100% salary increase from
    Dupont, leaves behind his pregnant wife,

    his team breeds 6,000 different strains of
    dusease resistent Wheat-including different
    varieties for each major climate on Earth.
  • 1945 -Discovers a way to grow Wheat

  • 1953 -crosses a short, sturdy dwarf breed

    Mexico wheat.
  • 1962 -Visits Delhi and brings his high-
    yieldind strains of wheat to the indian

    starvation due to a rapidly expanding
    population.
  • 1970 -receives the Nobel Peace Prize.
  • 1984 -become a distinguished professor
    at Texas A&M University.
  • 2005 -states "we will have to double the
    world food supply by 2050". Argues that
    genetically modified crops are the only way
    we can meet the demand, as we run out of
    arable land. Says that GM crops aren't
    inherently dangerous because "we've been
    genetically modifying plants and animals
    for a long time. Long before we called it
    science,people were selecting the best
    breeds."
  • 2009 -dies at the age of 95.
